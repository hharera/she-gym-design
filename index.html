The Unified UI & DRY Rule

One source of truth. One component, one implementation. Screens compose — they never restyle.

1 · Tokens

All colors, type, spacing, radius, motion live in tokens.css and lib/core/theme/. No hex literals or magic numbers in screens, ever.

2 · One component, one implementation

Each button, card, input, tile exists once in components.css (and matching Flutter widgets). Screens reference; they don't fork.

3 · No inline overrides

A new variant becomes a class. Inline style= attributes and re-skinned components are PR blockers.

4 · Cascade verified

A token change updates every screen automatically. The component gallery on this page is the smoke test.
